Unused or ignorable packets will simply not be listed.
That includes pretty much every packet sent by or to only the OSI GodClient.
1 byte variable types: byte, sbyte, char, uchar
2 byte variable types: short, ushort
4 byte variable types: int, uint
Byte, uchar, ushort, and uint are unsigned variables. Sbyte, char, short, and int are signed variables.

Note that 'creature' means a 'mobile' or 'character' - something which can walk around, etc. Creature is what those're called in SteamEngine.

Also note that 'thing' means either an item or a creature.
