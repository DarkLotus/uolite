This packet is not sent by "modern" clients, which send 0xad, the Unicode Speech Request packet, instead.

Blocksize should ALWAYS be checked against the length of the packet, and checked against a maximum length, so that if someone tries to send
you a 20000-letter speech, you'll disconnect them rather than proceeding to send it to every other client in hearing range.

Illegal characters should always be stripped from speech.

Speech Type
- Regular = 1
- Emote = 2 (:)
- Whisper = 3 (;)
- Yell = 4 (!)
