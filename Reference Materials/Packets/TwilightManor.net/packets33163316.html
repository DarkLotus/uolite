When this is sent, the move sequence number must be reset to 0 (on both server and client).

Colors have two flags, 0x4000 and 0x8000. 0x4000 enables transparency (Only transparent in the game-screen, appearing the normal color on the paperdoll (and, for items, and on the ground and in containers)). 0x8000 enables selective dyeing - The color only applies to the greys in the art. (Thus, if you have a GM body with a color 0x8021, you will look like someone wearing a GM robe dyed 0x21)

Flags should be (This may be incorrect, if so, please tell me):


  • 0x0001: Invul (Does client care?)
  • 0x0002: Dead
  • 0x0004: Poisoned
  • 0x0008: Yellow status bar?
  • 0x0010: Cannot Move (Does client care?)
  • 0x0020: Cannot Talk (Does client care?)
  • 0x0040: War mode
  • 0x0080: Hidden


Direction 0 is north, proceed clockwise for further directions. (1 is northeast, 2 is east, etc)
